Morogo (Stewed Greens)

30-45 MIN

Nutritions

Protein
~8 g
Fat
~10 g
Carbs
~10 g
Calories
~150 kcal

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 1 kg morogo (or spinach/greens), chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1–2 tbsp oil
  • Salt, pepper, lemon to taste
  • Optional: peanut butter or milk for creaminess

Quick steps

  1. Sauté onion, add tomatoes, then add greens and simmer until tender (15–25 min).
  2. Season and add a splash of milk or peanut butter if desired for richness.

Recipe — Morogo

1 — Prep

Wash and chop greens.

2 — Sauté

Sauté onion in oil until soft, add tomatoes and cook 2–3 minutes.

3 — Simmer

Add greens and a little water, cover and simmer until tender, 10–20 minutes. Season to taste.
