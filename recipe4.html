Vetkoek & Mince

30-45 MIN

Nutritions

Protein
~30 g
Fat
~30 g
Carbs
~60 g
Calories
~700 kcal

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder (quick vetkoek) or 2 tsp yeast
  • 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp sugar
  • Warm water ~1 cup
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 500 g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp curry powder, 1 tbsp tomato paste

Quick steps

  1. Mix dough, rest briefly (15 min for quick method), shape and deep-fry until golden.
  2. Sauté mince with onion, spices and tomato to make the filling (15–20 min).
  3. Split vetkoek and fill with mince; serve.

Recipe — Vetkoek & Mince

1 — Make dough

Mix flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Add enough warm water to form a soft dough. Rest 10–15 minutes.

2 — Prepare mince

Sauté onion, add mince and brown, stir in curry and tomato paste, simmer 10–15 minutes; season.

3 — Fry vetkoek

Heat oil to 170–180°C, shape dough into rounds and fry until golden (3–5 min each side). Drain on paper towel.
