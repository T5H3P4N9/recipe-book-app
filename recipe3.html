Chicken Curry

45-60 MIN

Nutritions

Protein
~35 g
Fat
~25 g
Carbs
~20 g
Calories
~500 kcal

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 600 g chicken pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 200 ml coconut milk (optional)
  • 1–2 tbsp oil, salt & pepper
  • Fresh coriander to garnish

Quick steps

  1. Sauté onion, garlic and ginger in oil until soft.
  2. Add curry powder and tomatoes; stir, add chicken and simmer 25–35 min.
  3. Finish with coconut milk if using; serve with rice.

Recipe — Chicken Curry

1 — Prep

Season chicken with salt and pepper.

2 — Sauté aromatics

Sauté onion, garlic and ginger in oil until soft (5–7 min).

3 — Add spices

Add curry powder and cook 1–2 min to bloom the spices.

4 — Add liquids

Add tomatoes and a little water or stock, bring to a simmer.

5 — Cook chicken

Add chicken pieces, simmer gently 25–35 minutes until cooked through.

6 — Finish

Stir in coconut milk (if using), warm through and adjust seasoning.
