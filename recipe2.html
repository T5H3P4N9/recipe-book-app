Tomato Smoor Boerewors Roll

30-40 MIN

Nutritions

Protein
~25 g
Fat
~20 g
Carbs
~50 g
Calories
~600 kcal

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 4 boerewors sausages
  • 4 rolls
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 400 g canned tomatoes or 4 fresh, chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste (optional)
  • 1 tsp sugar, salt & pepper
  • Chutney or relish to serve

Quick steps

  1. Make tomato smoor: sauté onion in oil, add tomatoes and simmer 10–15 min.
  2. Grill boerewors until cooked through (8–12 min), slice into rolls.
  3. Assemble rolls with smoor and chutney; serve.

Recipe — Tomato Smoor & Boerewors Roll

1 — Make smoor

Heat oil, sauté onions until translucent (4–5 min). Add tomatoes and tomato paste, reduce heat and simmer 10–15 minutes with sugar, salt and pepper.

2 — Cook boerewors

Prick and grill or pan-fry boerewors, turning to brown evenly, 8–12 minutes until cooked through.

3 — Assemble

Slice rolls, add a spoonful of smoor, place sliced boerewors and top with chutney or relish.
