Steamed Bread

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 500 g bread flour
  • 10 g active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 300 ml warm milk or water
  • 1 tsp salt

Quick steps

  1. Make a soft dough, knead and leave to prove 1–2 hours.
  2. Shape and steam the loaf(s) for 45–60 minutes until set.

Recipe — Steamed Bread

1 — Make dough

Mix flour, yeast, sugar and salt. Add warm milk and form a soft dough, knead 8–10 minutes.

2 — First prove

Cover and leave in a warm place until doubled (1–2 hours).

3 — Shape & steam

Shape dough into loaf, place in a greased pan and steam for 45–60 minutes until cooked through.
