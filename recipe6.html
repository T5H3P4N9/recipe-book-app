Samp & Beans

2–3 HRS

Nutritions

Protein
~20 g
Fat
~5 g
Carbs
~100 g
Calories
~600 kcal

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 2 cups samp (dried maize kernels)
  • 1 cup sugar beans (or other beans), soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp baking soda (optional to soften beans)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Quick steps

  1. Soak samp and beans (overnight), then simmer until tender (1.5–2.5 hrs).
  2. Sauté onion and tomatoes, add to cooked samp & beans and simmer to blend flavors.

Recipe — Samp & Beans

1 — Soak

Soak samp and beans overnight in plenty of water to shorten cooking time.

2 — Cook

Drain, cover with fresh water and simmer gently for 1.5–3 hours until tender, topping up water as needed.

3 — Finish

Sauté onion and tomatoes, add to cooked samp & beans and simmer 10–15 minutes to marry flavors; season to taste.
